## What is LetterBucket?

LetterBucket is a user-friendly newsletter mailing platform designed to help creators, entrepreneurs, and businesses grow and monetize their audience.

With a focus on simplicity and effectiveness, LetterBucket makes it easy to create, send, and optimize newsletters while ensuring compliance with email regulations.
